Know yourself and your surroundings to live better

Classrooms are often not monitored for environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, CO2 levels, or air pollutants, which may lead to discomfort, health issues, and reduced learning capacity. There is a lack of systems that continuously track these factors, meaning classrooms may not always offer the best conditions for effective teaching and learning. This project seeks to address this by developing a sensor-based system that monitors and improves classroom environments to ensure they remain clean, safe, and conducive to learning.

Our project is dedicated to creating a smarter, safer indoor environment using advanced sensor technology. By monitoring key factors such as air quality, temperature and humidity in real time, our system ensures that indoor spaces remain healthy and comfortable. With automated feedback and long-term data analysis, we provide actionable insights to maintain ideal conditions, promoting well-being and productivity. Join us in revolutionizing how we monitor and manage indoor environments for a better, healthier future.

AirSense empowers classrooms with a comprehensive network of sensors to deliver precise, real-time data on critical environmental factors. From air quality to temperature and noise levels, our system actively monitors and adapts to maintain a clean, safe, and optimal learning environment. By equipping educators and administrators with actionable insights, AirSense transforms classroom conditions to support student health, focus, and academic success.

About Us

We Are AirSense

AirSense is a pioneering project dedicated to enhancing indoor air quality and comfort in educational spaces. By utilizing a network of advanced sensors, we monitor critical environmental factors like air quality, temperature, humidity, and noise levels in real-time.
